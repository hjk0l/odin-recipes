How to make Cheesecake.

I will teach you how to make a cheesecake with these easy steps!

We will be taking ingredients that are easy to get to make a nice and tasty cheesecake.

Make sure that your ingredients are at room temp.

What ingredients do we need?

  • graham cracker dust
  • 32 oz cream cheese
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2/3 cups sour cream
  • 1 1/2 vanilla
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325F (160C).
  2. Prepare Graham Cracker crust and pur the crumbs into a 9" Springform pan and press firmly into the bottom and up the sides
    of your pan. Set aside.
  3. In the bowl of a stand mixer or in a large bowl (using a hand mixer) add cream cheese and stir until smooth and creamy (don’t over-beat or you’ll incorporate too much air).
  4. Add sugar and stir again until creamy.
  5. Add sour cream, vanilla extract, and salt, and stir until well-combined. If using a stand mixer, make sure you pause periodically to scrape the sides and bottom of the bowl with a spatula so that all ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  6. With mixer on low speed, gradually add lightly beaten eggs, one at a time, stirring just until each egg is just incorporated. Once all eggs have been added, use a spatula to scrape the sides and bottom of the bowl again and make sure all ingredients are well combined.
  7. Pour cheesecake batter into prepared springform pan. To insure against leaks, place pan on a cookie sheet that’s been lined with foil.
  8. Transfer to the center rack of your oven and bake on 325F (160C) for 50-60 minutes (or longer as needed, see note 3). Edges will likely have slightly puffed and may have just begun to turn a light golden brown and the center should spring back to the touch but will still be jello. Don't over bake or the texture will suffer.
  9. Remove from oven and allow to coll on top of the oven for 10 minutes. Once the 10 minutes passed, use a knife to gently loosen the crust from the inside of the springform pan. Do not remove the ring of the springform pan
  10. Allow cheescake to cool another 1-2 hours or until near room temperature before transferring to refigerator and allowing to cool overnight or at least 6 hours. Remove the ring of the springform pan just before serving then return it to the pan to store.
