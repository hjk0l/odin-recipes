How to Make a Pizza

Make This Easy Pizza Recipe!

Make this easy pizza with ingredients from home!

What Ingredients do we need?

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Heat your oven to 450-550Â°F
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet. Spread sauce, then add cheese and your preferred toppings.
  4. Slide the pizza onto the preheated stone or baking sheet. Bake for 8-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and enjoying.
