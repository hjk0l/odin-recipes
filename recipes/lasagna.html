How to Make Lasagna

Make this easy Lasagna recipe with easy steps!

With simple ingredients you can make tasty lasagana!

What Ingredients Do we Need?

  • 1 lb ground beef and/or Italian sausage
  • 3 jars pasta sauce (24 oz each)
  • 2 containers ricotta cheese (32 oz total)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper
  • 3 boxes oven-ready lasagna noodles
  • 8 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook and drain ground meat. Stir in pasta sauce and seasonings. Simmer.
  2. Combine ricotta, egg, Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. In a 9x13 pan, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat.
  4. Cover with foil, bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake 15 more minutes.
  5. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
